## [First complete Herculaneum scroll read using AI](https://scrollprize.org/firstscroll) ⭐️ 9.0/10

For the first time, an entire Herculaneum scroll has been read using AI and advanced imaging techniques, as announced by the Vesuvius Challenge team. This breakthrough unlocks ancient texts preserved by the eruption of Mount Vesuvius in 79 AD. This achievement demonstrates that AI can non-destructively read carbonized scrolls, opening the door to recovering hundreds of lost works from the Herculaneum library. It marks a new era in digital humanities and could revolutionize our understanding of ancient philosophy and literature. The scroll was read using micro-CT scanning and machine learning models trained to detect ink on carbonized papyrus. The Vesuvius Challenge, launched in March 2023, has awarded over $1.8 million in prizes to accelerate this research.
